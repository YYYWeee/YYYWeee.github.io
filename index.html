About

I'm a full stack software engineer with a Master degree of Management of Information System and work experience in CyberSecurity.

I started my career in Cybersecurity helping application strengthening its defense against malicious intruders. I enjoy what I do and I keep on learning every day to keep up with latest tools and best practices to enhance my capabilities. This role required me to work under high pressure and fast pace industry, which helps me develop my critical thinking ability and problem solving skill.

I'm captivated by the endless possibilities that coding offers in shaping the world around us. Software engineering is not just about writing lines of code; it's about solving complex problems, innovating, and making life more efficient and enjoyable through technology.

The prospect of creating software that can impact people's lives, whether by developing cutting-edge applications or contributing to advancements in fields like artificial intelligence and automation, is incredibly exciting to me. Moreover, the dynamic and ever-evolving nature of the industry means there's always room for growth and learning, which aligns perfectly with my passion for continuous self-improvement. Ultimately, becoming a software engineer allows me to use my endless imaginations to create things and value add to our society.
